FAQs

Q: What is the Mode of payment?
A: You can either use Paytm/Tez/Phone Pay Apps to pay for your order. Direct transfer into Bank account is also available.
Q: Do you provide Complete Framed Sketches also?
A: As of now, we do not provide this service. However, arrangements can be done and the cost of frame would be added.
Q: How early do I have to order so that it is delivered to me on time?
A: The order should be made 10 days prior to the date of delivery as shipping may take time.
Q: When is the Payment to be done?
A: All Payments will be taken prior to commencement of the order.
Q: Can changes be made, once I have already placed an order?
A: Any Change Requests in either image, size of Sketch, etc. will be entertained within first 2 days of the order.
