About

Kanika Dawalia

"Ever since I can remember,I have always been drawn to colors and paintings.I grew up watching my uncle who did Fine Arts in college make sketches and portraits and use different sorts of colors and techniques.Since an early age,I have always been a fan of drawing and painting.I used to paint for relaxation of mind and peace when all kinds of bad thoughts would hover my mind." "Sketching just happened out of sheer jealousy.I was in the first year of my undergraduate engineering degree program where I began to envy the artwork of a fellow friend.At first it seemed an impossible task but still I decided to give it a try.I pushed myself to a point where I could reach his level of expertise and then eventually out of practice I got better with each sketch.I started with simple black and white sketches of famous celebrities then moved on to add some color into them.Adding color really gives a new life to the sketch. Having received no formal training in the field of art,I present to you my raw talent. Hope you like it."
